More About Us

Page Mark

For 9 years, Page Mark has created and curated exceptional reputation from educators, librarians, and learners across the Bangladesh.
We are an educational solutions provider who facilitates the connection between contents and customers like academics & researchers that empowers them personally and professionally throughout their life. With more than Nine years of experience in providing quality content, Page Mark stands strongly today as one of the leading supplier of Educational Books & Resources for most of the Universities, Medical Collages, Research Institutes, Corporates and other Educational Institutes all across Bangladesh. We are continuously partnering with the world's best publishers, and societies to provide our esteemed clients from different fields of academic, medical, corporate, and libraries with world-class informational and knowledge solutions. Our spectrum of content has evolved from mere print book to include other means such as e-books, e-journals subscriptions, database in the ambit. Today, we are proudly considered one of the most preferred agent for import and supply of educational books and resources.
