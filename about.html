about us

Page Mark was established in the year 2013 with a futuristic objective to become a value-based, a technology-enabled organization facilitating the most qualitative learning content in Bangladesh. Page Mark has more than 9 years of experience in dealing with 5+ Global publishers serving different areas of knowledge. Today, we stand as the most preferred, go-to vendor for the major publishers, academic institutions, corporates, and research organizations. We take the opportunity to thank our publishing partners i.e. Cengage Learning, Taylor & Francis (Informa Uk Ltd.), Springer Nature, CAS (A division of American Chemical Society), Oxford University Press India etc. for their support on our activities and operations. Our strong ties with global publishing houses and new technology-driven accredited platforms help to enhance their educational knowledge and make research more credible. Page Mark is proud to be a respected name for Print & e-Books provider for most of the libraries across the country.
